About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to redefining the concept of cultural tourism in Egypt. Our goal is to create meaningful travel experiences that go beyond simple visits to historical sites, allowing visitors to truly live and interact with the rich heritage of Upper Egypt.

About Project

Heritopia is a cultural and interactive tourism project located in Luxor, Upper Egypt. It offers visitors a unique experience that allows them to explore and live the traditional lifestyle of Upper Egypt through activities, crafts, and cultural performances. The project aims to preserve heritage while presenting it in a modern and engaging way.
